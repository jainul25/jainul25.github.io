Educational Robotics & Kit Design

The ecosystem starts with hardware. I designed STEMbotix's hands-on robotics kits — ESP32-based boards, modular sensors and actuators, and a build-along curriculum — so a student goes from an unopened box to a working, programmable robot. Every kit is built to be classroom-deployable: safe, repeatable, and mapped to learning outcomes a teacher can actually run.

An adoptable ecosystem, not parts

The kit, the AI teacher, and the block-coding platform are one loop: a student builds the robot, codes it in blocks, gets unstuck by the AI teacher, and the instructor sees progress and runs the lesson. The whole experience is engineered for adoption — not a demo that sits on a shelf.

The Problem

Most ed-robotics is either hardware with no software story, or "AI" features that are ungoverned chatbots with no evals, guardrails, or measurable adoption. Schools need a complete, safe, scoped experience teachers will actually run.

My Role & Contributions

  • Designed the robotics kits — hardware, modular components & build-along curriculum
  • System architecture for the AI tutor + LMS + block-coding engine
  • Built eval harness & test sets; resolved duplicate test-case IDs
  • Authored guardrails for safe, age-appropriate AI output
  • Designed the kit → code → AI experience loop and instructor UI
  • Go-to-market: teacher enablement, school adoption, sales assets

Overview

A web app for internal team task management with a built-in scoring system — tasks are ranked by impact, effort, and urgency so the team always works the highest-value item next, instead of arguing about priorities.

The Problem

The team's work lived in scattered lists with no shared sense of priority. The scoring engine makes prioritization objective and repeatable — cutting planning overhead ~30% and reclaiming enough team time to save an estimated ~$45K/year in otherwise-lost hours.

My Role & Contributions

  • Product design and the scoring model (impact × effort × urgency)
  • Full webapp build — task CRUD, ranking, team views
  • Workflow design for daily/weekly planning
